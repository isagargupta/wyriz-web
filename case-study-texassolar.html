The Specific Problems

  • Manual Lead Qualification: Every incoming lead required 4-5 days of manual follow-up calls, emails, and data entry. 60% of leads fell through the cracks because they were contacted too late.
  • Inefficient Customer Support: 12 full-time employees were dedicated to answering customer inquiries. Many questions were repetitive and could be automated, but there was no system in place.
  • No Website Integration: Their website was primarily informational. Leads had to call or fill out a form, creating friction in the sales process.
  • Zero Pipeline Visibility: No centralized CRM. Sales data was scattered across spreadsheets, emails, and personal notes.
  • High Customer Acquisition Cost: With so many manual touchpoints, CAC was approximately €500 per customer, eroding profit margins.

Our Solution: The AI Automation Stack

We designed and implemented an integrated technology solution that automated the entire customer journey from first contact through close. Here is what we built:

1. AI Voice Agent - 24/7 Lead Qualification

Technology Stack: OpenAI GPT-4 + Twilio + Custom Node.js backend

Incoming customer calls are routed to our AI voice agent which can conduct full conversations in English and Spanish. The agent asks qualifying questions (property size, energy costs, timeline), books appointments directly into the calendar, handles common objections, and schedules automated follow-up calls.

Result: Call center staff reduced from 12 to 2 (handling only escalations). Cost savings: €240K/year. Lead response time dropped from 4-5 days to instant.

2. Intelligent Chatbot Layer - Omnichannel Support

Platforms: Website, WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger, Email

An AI chatbot on the homepage handles inquiries 24/7. Customers can message TexasSolar on WhatsApp and receive instant responses with product information and financing options.

Result: 85% of customer inquiries resolved without human intervention. Support response time: From 18+ hours to instant. Customer satisfaction increased.

3. Website Redesign & Conversion Optimization

We implemented a deeply integrated conversion engine. An interactive calculator allows users to enter roof size and current bill to generate a custom quote. Progressive profiling captures leads efficiently, and mobile optimization caters to the 70% of traffic coming from smartphones.

Result: Website traffic increased 45%. Conversion rate from visitor to qualified lead increased 340% in month one.

4. CRM Implementation & Process Automation

We centralized the database with Salesforce. Zapier workflows automate sequences—when a lead is qualified, the system sends welcome emails and texts. Leads are distributed automatically, contracts are generated instantly, and real-time dashboards give management 100% pipeline visibility.

Result: Manual data entry eliminated. Sales cycle reduced from 45 to 7 days.
